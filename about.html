This site is dedicated to the Game/AI Conference, the world's largest event focusing exclusively on artificial intelligence, gameplay and character animation. After four highly successful conferences in Paris from 2008 to 2011, the conference moved to the beautiful city of Vienna, Austria and returns 2014 for it's sixth edition.

The event is organized by AiGameDev.com.
