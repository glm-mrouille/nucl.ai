The nucl.ai™ conference grew out of the Game/AI Conference (in its 7th edition) as the largest worldwide event dedicated to its topic. The event brings together hundreds of professional developers, enthusiasts and practitioners from Europe and beyond!

Over the years, we've noticed best practices and techniques are often widely applicable to other industries, and our audience broadened considerably. This year the conference is switching to a multi-track format with multiple topics running in tandem during the same day.

This event is perfect for you if you're interested in digging into depth on business-critical subjects, stay up-to-date of upcoming technology, and establish contacts with other companies in the field. Join us in Vienna/Austria in July; tickets available from March 16th!
